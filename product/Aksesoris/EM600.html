EM600

Tombol Exit

General Description:

The single electromagnetic lock operates with holding forces of 600 lbs (250 kg) for EM600. Covering a range of applications, EM600 is ideal for applications from standard doors through to high use commercial swing doors. With fixing options including woods, aluminium and steel constructions, as well as glass door applications, the range caters to most electromagnetic needs.

Key Features:

  • Fail-safe: unlocks when power is removed
  • Easy installation: suitable for both new and retro usage
  • High holding force
  • Low current consumption
  • Silent operation: no humming or buzzing
  • Dual voltage option: 12 or 24 VDC
  • Instantaneous Release: smart electronics eliminate residual magnetism

Tehnical Specifications:

  • Holding Force EM600: 600 lbs (250 Kg)
  • Dimensions (L)238mm x (H)48mm x (D)27 mm
  • Voltage 12VDC/24VDC +/- 10%
  • Operating Temperature 0°C - 55°C
  • Operating Humidity : 0 - 95% (non condensing)
  • Indicator Built-In LED
  • Sensor Built-In Dry Contact
  • Weight EM600: 1.9 Kg
