EMB100

Tombol Exit

General Description:

The green breakglass marked Emergency Door Release should be installed on exit where access locks are installed in case of emergency. An optional plastic cover can be fitted in front of the breakglass ensuring that the breakglass cannot be activated accidently. The breakglass switch is held off by the edge of the glass. When the glass is broken, the switch will be released and triggered emergency release. A protective plastic coating on the glass prevents operator injury and a hammer to break the glass is not required. A special key to test the breakglass will be provided.

Key Features:

  • Suitable for Exit Door and Emergency Door
  • Protective cover to ensure the breakglass cannot be activated accidently
  • Hammer is not required to break the glass safely
  • Special key to test the breakglass

Tehnical Specifications:

  • Base Material : Heat resistant material
  • Dimensions : (L)85mm x (H)85mm x (D)50mm
  • Current Rating : 3A @36 VDC Maximum
  • Output Contact : Dry Contact NC/NO/COM
  • Operating Temperature : 0°C - 55°C
  • Operating Humidity : 0 - 95% (non condensing)
  • Weight : 0.2 Kg
